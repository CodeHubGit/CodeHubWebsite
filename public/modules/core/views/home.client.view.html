Isomer

The open source developer bootcamp.

Our mission

To create a community of tech evangelists with a passion for using technology to further social enterprise.

Open

Our cirriculum is open source, free, and available on github. At Isomer, we believe that open information and transparency are essential ingredients to a better tomorrow.

Community

We operate out of the Sustainable Startups co-working space, full of social entreprnuers. We also strongly encourage other developers and individuals who have gone through our program to return.

Giving Back

We offer a scholarships for under-represented groups in computer science. We also complete an open source project for a local non-profit every few months.
