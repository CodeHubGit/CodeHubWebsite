What's an Isomer?

An Isomer is a regular molecule with a alternative atomic structure. We take this metaphor to heart with each of our members. If you are clever, passionate, different, love learning, and want to change the world while doing so, then you're an Isomer.

Join a community of social entrepreunuers.

Operating out of Sustainable Startups' coworking space, you will be surrounded by a community of social entrepreneurs.

Fall in love with Technology.

If you're like us, you probably spend more time using your computer than any other tool in your life. At Isomer, we like to engage in an active dialogue about the role of technology is our lives, and many of it's social implication.

Become a leader and a team player.

Communicating with a product manager, other engineers, as well as dividing and managing work are essential aspects of software engineering leader. We teach the Scrum Agile process, as well as teach you how to coordinate a group with tools such as trello.

Core Engineering skills.

A computer is a very complicated system based of layers of abstraction. By definition, it is impossible to learn everything there is to know about such a complication system. At Isomer, we focus on teaching you how to solve original problems, in order to prepare your for the real world.

Use the most cutting-eddyiest tools around.

We are confident that the future is written in fullstack javascript. We focus primarily on the MeanJS framework, because we feel it is the best introduction to diverse software engineering concepts, easy to build large websites, and comprised of industry relevant tools.
